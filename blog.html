How to Prepare for Your Corporate Headshot Session

A professional headshot is your digital first impression, making proper preparation essential for optimal results. This comprehensive guide covers key aspects of headshot preparation, from wardrobe selection to posing techniques. Choose solid colors and well-fitted clothing that projects professionalism, while scheduling grooming appointments 1-2 weeks before. Practice your expressions and maintain good posture during the session. Book your appointment when you won't feel rushed, and arrive early to review goals with your photographer. Bring backup outfits and touch-up supplies, and stay hydrated. Clear communication about your objectives ensures photos that align with your professional brand.
