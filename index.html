Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a headshot session take?

Most individual sessions last 30-45 minutes, including time for outfit changes and different poses. We ensure you get a variety of professional shots without feeling rushed.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors. We'll provide detailed styling guidelines upon booking.

How many retouched photos will I receive?

Our standard package includes 3 fully retouched digital images of your choice, with options to purchase additional edits. All retouching is natural and professional.

When will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your proofs within 24 hours and your final retouched images within 3-5 business days after selecting your favorites.

Do you offer group rates for businesses?

Yes, we offer special rates for corporate teams of 5 or more. Contact us for custom packages and on-site photography options.
